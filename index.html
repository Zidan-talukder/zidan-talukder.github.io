I build intelligent systems at the intersection of robust mechatronics and Embodied AI. My foundation spans space autonomy (CanSat) and field robotics, where I engineered a Mars Rover science payload focused on life detection from soil and rock samples.

Building on this biological integration and my research in sEMG-controlled prosthetics, my focus is now evolving toward soft robotics and bio-inspired autonomy. I am passionate about engineering adaptable, biologically informed machines capable of safely interacting with unstructured environments. To fully transition into this domain, I am actively seeking funded Master's (MASc) programs or dedicated research opportunities (RA / Intern) to collaborate with labs pushing the boundaries of bio-robotics.
